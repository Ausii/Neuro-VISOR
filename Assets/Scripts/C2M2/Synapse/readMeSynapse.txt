**EMULATOR MOVEMENT**
To use the emulator movement with the mouse use right click to grab and hold right click down
to drag the object around

You can also free look while holding the object using (ctrl).

**Synapse**
To utilize the synapse you can automatically transform its position to the dendrites by
pressing "E"(Subject to change - STC).

This will make the synapse a child of the neuron cell, So to disconnect them from each other
press left mouse button (STC). 

You can also move the synapse freely if it is disconnected from the cell. Otherwise,
you can move the synapse relative to the neurons postion.
